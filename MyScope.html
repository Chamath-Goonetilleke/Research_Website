Traffic congestion presents a significant challenge in various countries, including Sri Lanka. The swift urbanization and increased use of motor vehicles in Sri Lanka have contributed to a rise in traffic jams, causing adverse effects on the environment, economy, and society. According to a report published by the World Bank, Colombo, the largest city in Sri Lanka, experiences an annual economic loss of approximately US$2 billion due to traffic congestion [1].

Traditional approaches for managing traffic flow at intersections involve either automated traffic light systems with fixed timings or manual control by a traffic police officer. In automated systems, predetermined timings govern each section of the intersection, and signals operate accordingly [2]

Consequently, the low-traffic section may not require as much time, while the high volume section may not receive sufficient time, resulting in operational inefficiencies [3]. Additionally, these issues can be exacerbated during different times of the day



Research Gap

Following areas are the research gaps found in most of the recent researches.

Accident Severity Detection

There are CCTV cameras, they are primarily used for monitoring traffic flow and not for detecting accidents. A research gap exists in developing an efficient and accurate real-time detection especially focusing on identifying accident severity levels

Emergency Vehicle Prioritization

A research gap exists in developing an advanced emergency vehicle prioritization system that utilizes innovative methods for visual and sound verification, ensuring accurate and prompt detection of emergency vehicles in congested traffic conditions.

Traffic Light Optimization

Smart traffic systems have been implemented in many countries to improve traffic flow. However, there have been relatively few research studies that consider all these parameters, including the number of vehicles, types of vehicles on the road, as well as the average waiting times for vehicles.

Traffic Violation Detection

There is a research gap in developing a comprehensive traffic violation monitoring system that utilizes advanced technologies, identify and penalize various types of traffic violations. This system should focus on enhancing road safety by deterring and penalizing violators



Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

Can deep learning-based traffic optimization with emergency vehicle prioritization alleviate congestion and enhance road safety in Sri Lankan urban areas?

Traffic congestion has been a growing problem affecting various industries worldwide for many years. Because most of Sri Lanka's traffic lights are pre-timed, the times for green and red are static, which causes congestion on most roads. Here a novel traffic algorithm will be used to optimize the traffic. By utilizing real-time data from vehicle detection and tracking, the algorithm determines optimal traffic signal timings, prioritizing the movement of vehicles and minimizing traffic congestion.

Proposed Solution

The study investigates the novel application of deep neural networks and image-processing technologies for intersection traffic control and emergency vehicle prioritizing, detecting violations of traffic laws and accident severity. The method combines Q-Learning and an optimization algorithm for effective traffic flow optimization. The comprehensive system includes a user-friendly desktop monitoring program that focuses on isolated intersection traffic flow and is accessible to authorized parties like the police department. Methodology

Figure 2. High Level Architecture of the system.

In our exploration strategy, we propose a creative answer for prioritizing emergency vehicles proficiently at crossing points. The cycle includes a few key stages: first, detecting the presence of an emergency vehicle and affirming the continuous emergency circumstance by noticing dynamic alarm lights or sounds.

Our system utilizes progressed computer vision and deep learning technologies, using OpenCV for video outline extraction and Just go for it (You Just Look Once) for vehicle detection and following. Live CCTV and receiver takes care of are handled through a Carafe server, starting the emergency vehicle detection module.

Also, our system optimizes traffic stream progressively, diminishing clog and holding up times. It gives exact vehicle acknowledgment and following, permitting versatile changes in accordance with traffic light timings. The easy to use web application goes about as a control center, empowering heads to design decisively positioned cameras.

Moreover, our examination digs into thorough traffic violation detection, consolidating quantitative information investigation for speed following and incorrect way driving with cutting edge deep learning methods like YOLOv3 for cap violation detection.
