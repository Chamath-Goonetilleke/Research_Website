How can advanced technologies optimize intersection traffic, prioritize emergency vehicles, detect accidents, alert services, and prevent violations for enhanced road safety?

Traffic congestion has been a growing problem affecting various industries worldwide for many years. Because most of Sri Lanka's traffic lights are pre-timed, the times for green and red are static, which causes congestion on most roads. Here a novel traffic algorithm will be used to optimize the traffic. Emergency Vehicle Prioritization

The primary aim of this study is to enhance the priority given to emergency vehicles, including ambulances, police vehicles, and firetrucks, within an intersection's traffic light system. The system's procedure involves identifying an emergency vehicle and verifying the presence of an emergency situation within that vehicle by inspecting both the siren lights and siren sound. If either the siren lights or siren sound is activated, the system deduces the existence of an emergency. In the event of an emergency vehicle with a confirmed emergency situation, the system proceeds to halt traffic in other lanes and facilitate the unobstructed passage of the emergency vehicle.
